About us!


We are a self-made, women-run, latina owned store! Our recipes are inspired by traditional latin deserts. We have been established since 2019. We have also won the San Digeo Best Desert competition. Our services vary to fit your needs! We have a variety of flavors, sizes, and products for you to choose from. We look forward to serving you and your families for whaterver you need!
